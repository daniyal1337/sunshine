    Specialities

    Specialties at Sunshine Hospital: Comprehensive Care Tailored to Your Needs

    Emergency Services 24*7

    Our 24/7 services are here for you around the clock. We provide continuous care, support, and assistance whenever you need it, day or night. Your convenience and peace of mind are our top priorities..

    ECG

    Our ECG (Electrocardiogram) services offer quick and precise heart monitoring. Whether it's day or night, our ECG services are available 24/7 to provide timely cardiac assessments for your peace of mind and health

    X-Ray Machine

    Experience the future of radiology with our 300 MA Digital (DR) X-Ray Machine. This advanced technology allows for high-quality digital imaging, providing clear and detailed X-ray results. With cutting-edge features and capabilities.

    Advance Physiotherapy Center

    Welcome to our Advanced Physiotherapy Center, where your wellness and rehabilitation are our primary focus. Our expert physiotherapists provide cutting-edge treatments and personalized care to help you regain mobility, reduce pain, and enhance your overall well-being.

    Pathology

    Step into our state-of-the-art Modular OT Complex, where surgical precision and patient safety converge. Our facility is equipped with the latest in modular operating room technology, ensuring a sterile and adaptable environment for various surgical procedures.

    Medical Experts

    We are dedicated to providing healthcare professionals with the ideal setting to deliver the highest standard of care while prioritizing patient well-being. Your health and safety are our top priorities.
